Pokémon – Game World Overview
Core Concept

Pokémon is a vibrant adventure world where people and magical creatures called Pokémon live and grow together. Players take on the role of a Pokémon Trainer, exploring regions, befriending Pokémon, training them, and battling other trainers to become the very best.

The heart of Pokémon is:

Friendship

Exploration

Growth

Strategy

Discovery

The World

Pokémon games take place across colorful regions, each inspired by real-world places. Every region contains:

Towns & cities

Wild areas (forests, caves, oceans, mountains)

Routes connecting locations

Gyms run by powerful trainers

Special landmarks (volcanoes, ruins, power plants, islands)

Each region has:

Its own Pokémon species

Local culture and buildings

A legendary or mythical Pokémon tied to its history

Exploration is encouraged:

Hidden items

Regions & Lands of the Pokémon Universe

The Pokémon world is made up of many unique regions, each inspired by real-world countries or environments. Every region has its own climate, culture, buildings, music, and Pokémon species. This makes exploration exciting and gives each game its own identity.
